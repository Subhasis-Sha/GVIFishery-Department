Vision & Mission

Vision

Ensuring sustainable fisheries development, welfare of fishermen communities, scientific aquaculture growth, and responsible management of aquatic resources in Odisha through innovation, environmental conservation, and modern fisheries practices.

Mission

To provide fishermen and fish farmers with a safe, secure, and sustainable environment for fisheries development.

To improve livelihood opportunities and socio-economic conditions of fisheries communities across Odisha.

To promote scientific fish farming, hatchery management, and sustainable aquaculture systems.

To strengthen fisheries infrastructure including fish landing centers, cold storage systems, and fish processing facilities.

To encourage responsible marine and inland fisheries management while protecting aquatic biodiversity and natural resources.

To support modernization of fisheries through advanced technologies, training programs, and research activities.

To increase fish production and productivity through sustainable utilization of water resources and scientific fisheries practices.

To create employment opportunities and encourage entrepreneurship in fisheries and aquaculture sectors.

To ensure the supply of hygienic, high-quality fish and fishery products for consumers and markets.

Major Policies:

  • Protection and welfare of fishermen communities across Odisha
  • Promotion of sustainable inland, marine, and aquaculture fisheries development
  • Strengthening fish hatcheries and fish seed production systems
  • Promotion of advanced fish farming and aquaculture technologies
  • Development and modernization of fisheries infrastructure including fish landing centers, cold chain systems, and fish markets
  • Improving fish production to meet growing domestic and commercial demand
  • Conservation and sustainable management of aquatic resources and biodiversity
  • Promotion of export opportunities for fish and value-added fisheries products
  • Generation of employment opportunities in fisheries and aquaculture sectors
  • Fisheries training, skill development, research, and technological advancement
