Blue Revolution and State Schemes

The following are the various State & Central Govt Schemes implemented by Department of Fisheries for the benefit of people of Odisha. These schemes can be availed by fisherman from Odisha to build a house/Tube well/Community Hall, avail insurance and Ban and Lean period Benefits. The Govt of Odisha will provide all the necessary support to fisherman who can avail these schemes and prosper in all walks of Life.

Disclaimer: The Images & Videos shown here are strictly for illustration purpose of the viewer. Actual unit or construction may vary based on requirement.

1. National Saving-cum-Relief Scheme (NFSRS) for Marine Fisherman

Unit Cost: Rs.3000/- per fisher per annum (to be shared equally between Centre and each of the general States).

Funding Pattern
CategoryShares
Central Gvt. Shares33%
State Gvt. Shares33%
Beneficiary Shares34%
Terms & Conditions
  • Beneficiary should be a fulltime active fisher,
  • Beneficiary should be a member of a functional local fishers cooperative society/ Federation/any other registered body.
  • Beneficiary should be Below Poverty Line (BPL) and of between 18 and 60 years of age,
  • Beneficiary fishers shall save Rs.1500/- over a period of 9 months.
  • States/ UTs may top up with any additional benefits.
  • States/ UTs shall be required to submit self contained project proposals.
  • The accumulated amount of Rs.4500/- shall be disbursed in 3 equal installments.
How to Apply

Beneficiary can apply for the scheme at the corresponding AD offices.

2. State Saving-cum-Relief Scheme (SFSRS) for Marine Fisherwoman

Unit Cost: Rs.3000/- per fisher per annum (to be shared by Odisha Govt).

Funding Pattern
CategoryShares
State Gvt. Shares33%
Beneficiary Shares67%
Terms & Conditions
  • Beneficiary should be a fulltime active fisherwoman,
  • Beneficiary should be a member of a functional local fishers cooperative society/ Federation/any other registered body.
  • Beneficiary should be Below Poverty Line (BPL) and of between 18 and 60 years of age,
  • Beneficiary fishers shall save Rs.1500/- over a period of 9 months.
  • Odisha Govt will disperse Rs 3000/- along with Beneficiary’s Rs 1500/- for a total of Rs 4500/-.
How to Apply

Beneficiary can apply for the scheme at the corresponding AD offices.

Unit Cost: Rs 5000/- per marine fishermen family.

5. Build a House - only for Fisherman community (Central Scheme)

Unit Cost: Construction of new houses @ Rs.1,20,000/- per house

Funding Pattern
SharesCategory
State Gvt. Shares100%
Terms & Conditions
  • The housing unit should have a minimum plinth area of 25 Sq.m including sanitation facility.
  • Beneficiaries should be actively involved in fishing activities.
  • Preference to be given to fishers Below Poverty Line (BPL).
  • Beneficiaries owning kutcha houses can also be considered.
  • A cluster based development of not less than 20 houses should be adopted.
How to Apply

Beneficiary can apply for the scheme at the corresponding AD offices.

6. Build a House - only for Fisherman community (State Scheme)

Unit Cost: Construction of New houses @ Rs.1,70,000/- per house (State Govt Share: Rs.1,10,000/-, Central Govt Share: Rs.60,000/-)

Funding Pattern
SharesCategory
Central Gvt. Shares35%
State Gvt. Shares65%
Terms & Conditions
  • Beneficiaries should be actively involved in fishing activities.
  • The housing unit should have a minimum plinth area of 25 Sq.m.
  • Beneficiary needs Single Patta or Group Patta of the land.
  • Beneficiary should not have benefitted previously from any Govt housing schemes.
How to Apply

Beneficiary can apply for the scheme at the corresponding AD offices.

7. Build a Tube Well - Drinking Water Facility for Fisherman

Unit Cost: Unit cost of tube well @ Rs. 0.50 lakh

Funding Pattern
SharesCategory
State Gvt. Shares100%
Terms & Conditions
  • One tube-well shall be provided for each cluster of a minimum of 20 houses.
  • Alternative sources may be provided if tube-wells are not feasible.
How to Apply

Beneficiary can apply for the scheme at the corresponding AD offices.

8. Build a Community Hall for Fisherman

Unit Cost: Rs.4.00 lakh per unit.

Funding Pattern
SharesCategory
State Gvt. Shares100%
Terms & Conditions
  • Clusters/ Fishers' Villages with 75 or more houses shall be eligible.
  • The Community Hall should have a minimum plinth area of 200 sq.m.
  • Post construction maintenance shall be vested with the Village Panchayats.
How to Apply

Beneficiary can apply for the scheme at the corresponding AD offices.

9. Group Accident Insurance for Active Fishermen

Unit Cost: Rs.4.00 lakh per unit.

Funding Pattern
SharesCategory
State Gvt. Shares100%
Terms & Conditions
  • Licensed/ registered fishers shall be eligible for insurance for Rs. 2.00 lakh against death or permanent total disability.
  • The insurance cover shall be for a period of 12 months.
  • This component shall be implemented through FISHCOPFED.
How to Apply

Beneficiary can apply for the scheme at the corresponding AD offices.
