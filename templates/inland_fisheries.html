Inland Fisheries

Odisha possesses extensive inland water resources including rivers, reservoirs, ponds, lakes, wetlands, canals, and seasonal water bodies which provide significant opportunities for inland fisheries and aquaculture development.

GVI Fishery, Bhubaneswar, Odisha promotes sustainable inland fisheries through scientific fish culture methods, hatchery development, fish seed production, and modern aquaculture systems.

The organization focuses on increasing fish production by utilizing available inland water resources through expansion, intensification, diversification, and sustainable fisheries management practices.

Modern fish farming techniques including Biofloc systems, cage culture, integrated fish farming, ornamental fisheries, and recirculatory aquaculture systems are encouraged for improving productivity and livelihood opportunities.

GVI Fishery also supports fishermen and fish farmers through awareness programs, training initiatives, technical guidance, and infrastructure development for sustainable fisheries growth in Odisha.

The following are the major Inland Fisheries activities promoted by GVI Fishery, Bhubaneswar, Odisha.

Schemes:

  • Fish Seed Production and Hatchery Development
  • Inland Fish Production in Reservoirs and Water Bodies
  • Integrated Fish Farming with Agriculture and Livestock
  • Cage Culture and Biofloc Fish Farming Systems
  • Ornamental Fisheries Development
  • Sustainable Aquaculture and Fisheries Training Programs
  • Fisheries Infrastructure and Pond Development
  • Fish Farmers Development and Technical Support
  • Conservation and Sustainable Fisheries Management

Special Scheme(s):

  • Scientific Fish Culture in Farm Ponds
