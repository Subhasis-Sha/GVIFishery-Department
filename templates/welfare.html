GVIFishery WELFARE BOARD

GVIFishery Welfare Board, Bhubaneswar, Odisha was established with a vision to ensure social security, sustainable livelihood, and welfare support for fishermen, fish farmers, and workers engaged in fisheries and allied activities across the State. The Board functions under the guidance of the Department of Fisheries and Animal Resources Development, Government of Odisha, and works towards improving the socio-economic conditions of traditional fishing communities through various welfare initiatives and development programmes. The Board is administered by senior officials and nominated members who monitor and supervise welfare activities and fisheries development schemes throughout Odisha.

Organogram

A large number of fishermen, fish vendors, aquaculture workers, and fisherwomen from coastal and inland regions of Odisha are enrolled as beneficiaries under the Welfare Board to receive financial assistance, insurance support, educational benefits, and livelihood development opportunities.

The Department of Fisheries, Government of Odisha, always accords high priority to the welfare and development of fishermen and fisheries workers in the State through various financial assistance and social security schemes. To ensure transparency, efficiency, and timely delivery of benefits, financial assistance under different welfare programmes is directly transferred to the bank accounts of beneficiaries through secure digital payment systems and Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) mechanisms.
