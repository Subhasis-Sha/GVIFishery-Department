Director's Desk:

It is my privilege to welcome you to the GVIFishery Department, Bhubaneswar, Odisha. The fisheries sector plays a vital role in the socio-economic development of Odisha by providing livelihood opportunities to thousands of fishermen and supporting employment in fish farming, fish processing, transportation, and marketing sectors. Odisha is richly blessed with vast marine, brackish water, riverine, and inland fishery resources suitable for both capture and culture fisheries.

Odisha has a long coastline along the Bay of Bengal and abundant inland water resources including rivers, reservoirs, ponds, and wetlands that contribute significantly to fish production in the state. The fisheries sector supports the livelihood of lakhs of fisherfolk families and contributes greatly towards food security, nutritional development, and economic growth. The state also plays an important role in seafood production and export activities.

The Government gives top priority to the welfare and development of fishermen communities through various welfare schemes, financial assistance programs, modern fishing facilities, and skill development initiatives. The GVIFishery Department focuses on adopting advanced technologies, scientific fish farming methods, sustainable aquaculture practices, and modern infrastructure to increase fish production and improve the income of fisherfolk across Odisha.

The Department is committed to the conservation and sustainable management of fishery resources. Effective implementation of fisheries regulations, promotion of eco-friendly fishing practices, and protection of aquatic biodiversity are among the key objectives of the Department. To strengthen the fisheries sector, the Government continuously invests in fish landing centers, cold storage facilities, hatcheries, fish seed production units, fish markets, and modern fishing harbours for the benefit of the fishing community.

The GVIFishery Department, Bhubaneswar, Odisha remains dedicated to empowering fisherfolk, enhancing fish production, ensuring sustainable resource utilization, and contributing towards the overall economic and social development of the state.
